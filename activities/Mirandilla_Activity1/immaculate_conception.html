FEAST OF THE IMMACULATE CONCEPTION

December 8, 2025



Brief Description:

The Feast of the Immaculate Conception of Mary is celebrated on December 8 each year. In the Philippines, it is recognized as a special non-working holiday under Republic Act No. 10966. This Catholic feast honors the belief that the Virgin Mary was conceived without original sin. Celebrations typically include Masses, processions, and other religious and cultural activities to venerate Mary as the mother of Jesus.
